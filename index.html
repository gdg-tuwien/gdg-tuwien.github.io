about

We are the first official, student-led campus chapter of Google's worldwide developer network in Vienna, Austria.

We achieve this through events such as reading groups / journal clubs and speaker series.

These events span various topics within computer science and maths, with a strong focus on machine learning and data systems.

events

Our events are open to absolutely anyone and free to attend!

We organize ourselves via a Google Groups mailing list.

Becoming a Volunteer: Anyone can join our team and help out with events.

  • Provide: volunteering service (e.g. event planning, marketing and communications).
  • Benefit from: volunteering certificates and reference letters.

Becoming an Organizer: For those who want to take on more responsibility.

  • Provide: regular volunteering and commitment to responsibilities
  • Benefit from: recognition through official GDG Co-Organizer Status, invitation to exclusive Google events and channels with Googlers or Google Developer Experts, a direct point of contact to a regional lead or community manager providing support, access to the "Google Community Leads" resource hub with technical content and guided training.
