about

In short: We're a graduate student club that likes to throw geeky events.

Hello World! We are the first official, student-led campus chapter of Google's worldwide developer network in Vienna, Austria.

Our community primarily consists of graduate students studying computer science or mathematics. We strive to create an inclusive space aimed at (1) promoting collaboration between top researchers and industry professionals and (2) helping students transition from academia to industry.

We achieve this through events such as speaker series, academic paper reading groups and casual social gatherings. These events span various topics within computer science, with a strong focus on machine learning, statistical learning and data systems.

join our team

We've just gotten started and have opportunities for everyone to get involved and make a difference.

Becoming a Volunteer: Anyone can join our team and help out with events.

  • Provide: volunteering service (e.g. event planning, marketing and communications)
  • Benefit from: volunteering certificates and reference letters

Becoming an Organizer: For those who want to take on more responsibilities.

  • Provide: regular volunteering and commitment to responsibilities
  • Benefit from: recognition through official GDG Co-Organizer Status, invitation to exclusive Google events and channels with Googlers or Google Developer Experts, a direct point of contact to a regional lead or community manager providing support, access to the "Google Community Leads" resource hub with technical content and guided training.

If any of this sounds fun to you and you're passionate about community building or simply want to be part of something exciting, we'd love to have you on board!

Just reach out via Email or Whatsapp @ sueszli.github.io.
